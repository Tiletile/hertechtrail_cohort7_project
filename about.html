MY TECH JOURNEY

My Journey into tech started in 2022 through a free web development class organized by Mrs Habeebah Aleilo (The Queen of Web) which I showed interest in, lots of things discouraged and hindered the learning process. The passion was there but the main set back was that I never had a Laptop to code and practice. However, I started coding on Mobile phone using (TrebEdit App) and I was able to develop a website with it.see below... 👇

Project done with my mobile phone

After the training ended I got certified in completion of HTML AND CSS october 2022. The training advanced to JavaScript and i couldn't participate because I do not own a Laptop then. below is the certificate of completion

In 2023, About Few months ago, I applied for HertechTrail cohort7 Program for web development[FrontEnd] with the intention of using my phone to code which I eventually got selected . While reading the terms and I saw that Laptop is a requirement before the training, I felt sad because I own none. I discussed the setback with my mentor and he offered to lend me his Laptop for the time being and he did. Few weeks after commencement of the training, I stumbled on an anonymous on facebook on a friend's page I sent him an anon message that I'm in need of a laptop for my tech training then boom, my message got delivered to him and he helped me with a laptop which I repaired and started using it for the training. HertechTrail Academy then helped set the pace. It's been beautiful journey I must say!

Thanks to HertechTrail Academy both the founder [Ms. Gloria Ojukwu] and energetic coach [Edozie Magdalene Princess (PrincessMaggy)] for taking me through this beautiful Journey.
